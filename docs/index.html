Listify

Listify is a tool to convert a Spotify playlist into a simple text list of tracks.
Give the list to a party DJ, E-mail it to your friends or whatever!

Please login with your Spotify account to continue.



Note: Listify will only fetch up to the first 50 playlists from your account and up to 100 tracks per playlist.
